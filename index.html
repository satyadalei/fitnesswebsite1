THE FACILITY

The WARDIERE team spent a significant amount of time brainstorming, planning, designing, and developing the 4,200 square feet that make up this centrally-located performance training facility.

The sketch (right) is one of the final drawings leading up to the actual buildout of Output Performance. Our facility was designed for team training, general fitness members, and amateur and professional athletes. Our goal: create a division 1 environment featuring top-of-the-line equipment and experienced guidance to challenge and push individuals as well as properly prepare athletes for the next step in their amateur or professional career.
